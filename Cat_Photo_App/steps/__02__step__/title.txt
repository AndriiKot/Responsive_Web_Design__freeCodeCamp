Step  2

The h1 through h6 heading 
elements are used to signify the importance 
of content below them. The lower the 
number, the higher the importance, so h2 
elements have less importance than h1 elements.
Example 
Code&lt;h1&gt;most important heading element&lt;/h1&gt;
&lt;h2&gt;second most important heading 
element&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;third most important heading element&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;h4&gt;fourth most important 
heading element&lt;/h4&gt;
&lt;h5&gt;fifth most important heading element&lt;/h5&gt;
&lt;h6&gt;least important 
heading element&lt;/h6&gt;

Only use one h1 element per 
page and place lower importance headings below 
higher importance headings.
